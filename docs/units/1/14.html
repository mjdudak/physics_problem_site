Unit 1

1.14 Calculating the Motion of One Object

Directions: In each of the following questions, do the calculation asked of you. You can use math, graphs, motion maps, or any other method to solve the problem. Please show your work.

1.14.1) A dog starts at a position of 10 m. It travels for 3 seconds at 5 m/s in the positive direction. Where will the dog's position be after the 3 seconds?

1.14.2) A train is travelling at a speed of 20 m/s. How far has it gone after 30 seconds?

1.14.3) An asteroid is hurtling through space at 17.9 km/s. If the asteroid is pointed directly at Earth and is 10,000 km away, how long until it hits Earth?

1.14.4) You wake up late for school and have missed the bus. You must run 1 mile (about 1600 meters) to make it to school. You only have 10 minutes to make it. How fast do you have to run?

1.14.5) A car starts at an unknown position. It travels 15 seconds, during which it moves with a constant velocity. You measure the car moving at about 20 m/s, and it ends at a position of 45 meters. Where did it start?
