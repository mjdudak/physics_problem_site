Unit 1

1.3 Description to v-t

Directions: In each of the following problems, create a velocity vs. time graph from the following descriptions. Your graphs only need to be qualitatively correct (meaning exact numbers do not matter).

1.3.1) A car starts at a position of -10 m and moves in the positive direction at 2 m/s for 10 s.

1.3.2) A person starts at a position of 5 m and walks in the negative direction at -4 m/s for 3 s.

1.3.3) A ball rolled from a position of 7 m. It rolls in the positive direction at a constant speed of 0.5 m/s for 10 s.

1.3.4) A boat starts at the origin and moves in the positive direction at a speed of 12 m/s for 6 s.

1.3.5) A bike starts at a position of -3 m and moves in the negative direction at a speed of 8 m/s for 10 s.
