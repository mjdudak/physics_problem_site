Unit 1

2.2 Guided 2-object intersections

Directions: In each of the following problems, follow the steps to find where the objects meet.

2.2.1) A toy car is moving at a speed of 2 m/s to the left. A ball is rolled toward the toy car at a speed of 3 m/s. If the car and the ball start a distance of 40 m apart, where are they when they collide?

2.2.2) Two asteroids are headed toward each other. They are initially 2000 km apart. If one is travelling at 21 km/s and the other is travelling at 15 km/s, where do they meet?

2.2.3) A jaguar is stalking its prey, walking behind a capybara. When the jaguar is 50 m away, the capybara is spooked and starts running away at 9 m/s. The jaguar pursues the capybara, running after it at a speed of 22 m/s. How far does the capybara go before meeting its doom?

2.2.4) When driving, you encounter a slower car. You are travelling at a speed of 25 m/s, and they are going 20 m/s. When the front of your car is about 7m behind the other car, you move into the passing lane. How far have you gone when the front of your car is in line with the front of their car (assume their car is 7m long)?

2.2.5) Two runners, Juliana and Rosa, are on the last stretch of the race. Juliana is sprinting, going 4 m/s, but she is behind Rosa by 10m. Rosa is going a slower 3.5 m/s and has 100m of the race to finish. Does Juliana catch up?
